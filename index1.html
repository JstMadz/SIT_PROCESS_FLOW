Forms Repository
Step 1

Document Submission & Checklist

Accomplish and submit the following documents in two (2) long brown envelopes.

Original Envelope

  • Originally signed Application Form
  • Originally signed Waiver Form
  • Copy of Govt ID (Parent) w/ 3 signatures
  • Original Medical Clearance Slip
  • Original NBI Clearance

Duplicate Envelope

  • Originally signed Application Form
  • Originally signed Waiver Form
  • Copy of Govt ID (Parent) w/ 3 signatures
  • Photocopy of Medical Clearance Slip
  • Photocopy of NBI Clearance
Application Form Waiver Form
Step 2

Verification & Return

Once signed by your SIT Coordinator, Department Head, and Director of the IRJP:

  • Original Envelope: Returned to the student.
  • Duplicate Envelope: Kept by the coordinator.
Step 3

HTE Selection & Validation

Select a Host Training Establishment (HTE) through one of two methods:

Option A: Partner HTE
Choose from companies with an existing MOA.
Option B: Personal Choice
Apply elsewhere.
Coordinator must validate legitimacy first.
Step 4

Application Process

If a suitable company is found:

1. Request Recommendation

Request Form (GForm)

2. Submit to Company

  • • Signed Recommendation Letter
  • • Curriculum Vitae (CV) / Resume
  • • Letter of Intent
Step 5

Company Acceptance

Upon acceptance, secure the following:

  • Acceptance Letter
  • Training Plan (Company or Univ Format)
Training Plan Template
Step 6

University Endorsement

  1. Fill out Request for Endorsement.
  2. Coordinator issues signed Endorsement Letter.
  3. Submit a copy of received letter back to Coordinator.
Endorsement Form (GForm)
Step 7

Acceptance Monitoring

Fill out this form strictly for tracking purposes once accepted.

Monitoring Form
Step 8

MOA & Training Agreement

Required for new partners, companies without MOA, or expired MOAs.

  • Print 5 copies on Long/Legal Bond Paper.
  • Submit to Coordinator for Univ Signing.
  • Student/Coordinator facilitates Company Signing & Notarization.
MOA Template Training Agreement Template
