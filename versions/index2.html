TECHNOLOGICAL UNIVERSITY OF THE PHILIPPINES

College of Science

Bachelor of Science in Information Systems

Supervised Industrial Training

Official process flow for application, endorsement, and training commencement.

Step 1

Document Submission

Prepare two brown envelopes (Original & Duplicate) containing the required documents.

Original Envelope

  • Original Signed Application Form
  • Original Signed Waiver Form
  • Copy of Parent/Guardian ID (3 signatures)
  • Original Medical Clearance (Univ. Clinic)
  • Original NBI Clearance

Duplicate Envelope

  • Original Signed Application Form
  • Original Signed Waiver Form
  • Copy of Parent/Guardian ID (3 signatures)
  • Photocopy Medical Clearance
  • Photocopy NBI Clearance
Step 2

Verification & Return

Once signed by the SIT Coordinator, Department Head, and IRJP Director:

  • Original Envelope: Returned to the student.
  • Duplicate Envelope: Kept by the coordinator for safekeeping.
Step 3

HTE Selection & Validation

Select a Host Training Establishment (HTE) through one of two methods:

Option A: Partner HTE
Choose from the list of companies with existing MOA provided by the Coordinator.
Option B: Personal Choice
Apply to other companies. Note: Coordinator must validate the company's legitimacy first.
Step 4

Application Process

Once a company is selected/approved:

  1. Request a Recommendation Letter from the Coordinator.
  2. Submit the following to the chosen Company:
    • Signed Recommendation Letter
    • Curriculum Vitae (CV) / Resume
    • Letter of Intent (Signed by student)
Step 5

Company Acceptance

If accepted, the student must secure:

Acceptance Letter
Training Plan (from Company OR Coordinator's template)
Step 6

University Endorsement

Upon receiving the Acceptance Letter or email screenshot:

1
Student fills out the Request for Endorsement Form (GForm) provided by coordinator.
2
Coordinator issues Endorsement Letter signed by the IRJP Director.
3
Student submits a copy of the received Endorsement Letter back to the Coordinator.
Step 7-8

MOA Execution (New & Expiring Partners)

This step is required specifically for new partner HTEs, companies without an existing MOA, or companies with expired/expiring MOAs.

Process Checklist:

  • Use University MOA Template.
  • Print 5 Copies on Long Legal Bond Paper.
  • University Signing: Submit to Coordinator → Signed by College Dean & Director.
  • Company Signing: Facilitated by Student or Coordinator.
  • Notarization: Facilitated by the Student.
